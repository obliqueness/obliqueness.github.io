WritChal24

WritChal24 is a writing challenge created by Alice, Laura, and many others!

Here are the guidelines:

TENETS OF WRITCHAL24 [MUTATE TO YOUR HEARTS CONTENT]

  • Write every day (or whatever routine you want to establish) I am doing once a week [at least] because I am trying not to destroy myself.
  • Post it online everyday (if you are comfortable).
  • Based on weekly themes that have yet to be fully decided.
  • Can write about the same thing multiple times, but focus on being novel / pushing yourself.
  • Structure is left as an exercise for the author.
  • Build off of others entries (ooo interaction yum)
